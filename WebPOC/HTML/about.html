What is Blueprint.VIU.CS?

Blueprint.VIU.CS is a user-friendly program that helps computer science students manage their workloads on a per semester basis, focusing on helping students plan the easiest class combination possible to achieve their goals. The idea is that students can select the courses they want for a particular semester, and it will come up with course specific information and an overall semester difficulty score, which tells students whether or not they should change their courses. The target audience of our project will be VIU CS students who want to ensure success in a specific semester and minimize the chance of failing classes due to a high semester workload.
